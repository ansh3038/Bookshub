The Fastest Man Alive


   

The Fastest Man Alive : The True Story of Usain Bolt

In this autobiography, Bolt himself shares how, as he grew up and played cricket and soccer, he and others learned he could run fast.

This beautifully illustrated autobiography shares the story from Bolt's perspective. It is a celebration of someone who was inspired by other athletes around the world, someone who worked for years to become the best at his sport. Bolt shares stories of the sacrifices he made, the influence of Cristiano Ronaldo, the power of soccer and dancehall music, and his signature lightning bolt move.

Author :  Usain Bolt
Language :  English
Publisher :  Sports Publishing (October 1, 2012)
Pages :  288
