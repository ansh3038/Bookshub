I AM Malala


   

I Am Malala: The Girl Who Stood Up for Education and Was Shot by the Taliban

I Am Malala: The Story of the Girl Who Stood Up for Education and was Shot by the Taliban is an autobiographical book by Malala Yousafzai, co-written with Christina Lamb.

Malala Yousafzai is a Pakistani activist for female education and the youngest-ever Nobel Prize laureate. She is known forhuman rights advocacy, especially education of women in hernative Swat Valley in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, northwest Pakistan,where the local Taliban had at times banned girls from attendingschool.

Author :  Malala Yousafzai and Christina Lamb
Language :  English
Publisher :  Weidenfeld & Nicolson (UK) Little, Brown and Company (US)
Pages :  288
