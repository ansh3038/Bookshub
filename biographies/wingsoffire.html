Wings of Fire

The 'Wings of Fire' is an autobiography by visionary scientist Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, who from very humble beginnings rose to be the President of India.

This is a story of his carrer as a defene scientist and his struggle as well as the story of Agni, Prithvi, Nag and Akash missiles which increased India's missile power and gave him a name missile man of India. This is also a saga of independent India's strggle for self sufficiency and defensive autonomy.

Author :   A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, Arun Tiwari
Language :  English
Publisher :  Universities Press
Pages :  180
